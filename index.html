CLIFFORD JOSE NEDIYAPARAMBIL

Clifford Jose Nediyaparambil is a multidisciplinary individual whose journey spans philosophy, language, art, technology, and literature. Formerly a seminarian and philosophy student, he is now an aspiring software engineer, combining analytical thinking with creativity and intellectual depth.

He pursued philosophical studies at St. Thomas Apostolic Seminary, Vadavathoor, Kottayam, where he underwent academic and spiritual formation. During this period, he made a conscious decision to step away from seminary life and explore a broader path shaped by intellectual, creative, and technological interests.

He has achieved advanced proficiency in the German language, completing A1 to B2 levels at Goethe-Zentrum Trivandrum and advancing to C1 level at the Goethe-Institut Mumbai, strengthening his communication and intercultural skills.

Beyond academics, he actively engages in chess and is involved in the international watercolor community. He also serves as a Brand Ambassador for KUKOYO Camlin.

He is currently working on his debut novel, Unread at 2:17 AM, exploring themes of silence, timing, and modern relationships.

At the core of his work lies the belief that art, thought, and technology are deeply interconnected.
