CLIFFORD JOSE NEDIYAPARAMBIL

Clifford Jose Nediyaparambil is a multidisciplinary individual whose journey spans philosophy, language, art, technology, and literature. Formerly a seminarian and philosophy student, he is now an aspiring software engineer, bringing together analytical thinking, creativity, and intellectual depth.

He pursued philosophical studies at St. Thomas Apostolic Seminary, Vadavathoor, Kottayam, where he was engaged in academic and spiritual formation. During the course of his philosophy studies, he made a conscious decision to step away from seminary life, choosing a path shaped by broader intellectual, creative, and technological exploration.

His academic background includes advanced proficiency in German, having completed A1 to B2 levels at Goethe-Zentrum Trivandrum and C1 level at the Goethe-Institut Mumbai. This linguistic training has strengthened his critical thinking, intercultural awareness, and ability to engage in structured academic discourse.

Beyond academics, he is actively involved in online chess platforms, reflecting his interest in strategy, discipline, and logical reasoning. He is also an active participant in the international watercolor community. In recognition of his artistic involvement, he serves as a Brand Ambassador for the KUKOYO Camlin Company.

Alongside his academic and creative pursuits, he is currently working on his debut novel, Unread at 2:17 AM, exploring themes of silence, timing, emotional distance, and modern relationships.

At the core of his work lies the belief that art, thought, and technology are deeply interconnected.
