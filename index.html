CLIFFORD JOSE NEDIYAPARAMBIL

Clifford Jose Nediyaparambil is a multidisciplinary individual whose journey spans philosophy, language, art, technology, and literature. Formerly a seminarian and philosophy student, he is now an aspiring software engineer combining analytical thinking with creativity.

He pursued philosophical studies at St. Thomas Apostolic Seminary, Kottayam, and later chose a broader intellectual and technological path.

He has completed A1–B2 German at Goethe-Zentrum Trivandrum and C1 training at Goethe-Institut Mumbai, strengthening his communication and global perspective.

He is active in chess, watercolor art, and serves as a Brand Ambassador for KUKOYO Camlin.

He is currently writing his debut novel Unread at 2:17 AM, exploring modern emotional themes.
